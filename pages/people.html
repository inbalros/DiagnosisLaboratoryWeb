Dr. Roni Stern is a senior lecturer in the Software and Information Systems Engineering (SISE) department at Ben Gurion University of the Negev. Prior to joining BGU, he was a post-doctoral fellow at Harvard University. He obtained his Ph.D. from Ben Gurion University and his M.Sc. from Bar-Ilan University. His main research interests are multi-agent planning, heuristic search, and automated diagnosis. He currently serves as the president of the international Symposium on Combinatorial Search (SoCS), and as the co-chair of the best paper in sister conference track in IJCAI 2019.
